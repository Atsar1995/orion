# Executive Summary

ORION is an AI-first Business Operating System (AI-BOS) designed to connect, automate and intelligently manage every aspect of a modern business.

Rather than replacing existing business software, ORION integrates with it, providing a single intelligent workspace where business owners can monitor operations, automate workflows and make better decisions.

The long-term vision is to create the world's most trusted AI Business Operating System.
