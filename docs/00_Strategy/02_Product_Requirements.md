## Product Overview

ORION is an **AI Business Operating System** — a unified platform that connects the business applications organizations already use and delivers one intelligent workspace to run, understand, and grow the entire operation.

Most businesses do not need more software. They need their existing software to work together. ORION sits above the stack — property management, e-commerce, accounting, marketing, messaging, and analytics — ingesting data in real time, applying AI to surface what matters, and enabling action without constant context-switching.

From a single workspace, operators can monitor performance, automate repetitive workflows, ask questions in natural language, and make decisions backed by live, cross-system data. ORION does not replace the tools businesses depend on. It connects them, orchestrates them, and makes them intelligent as one.

**Product positioning:** One AI. One Workspace. Complete Business Control.

## Customer Problems

### 1. Constant context-switching between systems

Operators open five to ten applications before their first coffee. Reservations in one tab. Sales in another. Accounting in a third. Each switch costs time, attention, and momentum.

### 2. Data trapped in silos

Revenue in the PMS. Ad spend in Meta. Customer conversations in WhatsApp. Financials in accounting software. No single system holds the full picture — and none of them agree without manual reconciliation.

### 3. Marketing disconnected from operations

Campaigns run without visibility into inventory, occupancy, or margin. Marketing teams and operations teams work from different realities, leading to wasted spend and missed opportunities.

### 4. No single source of truth

Business owners lack one clear dashboard that answers the questions that matter: *How are we performing today? Where is revenue leaking? What needs attention right now?*

### 5. Repetitive manual work at scale

Daily tasks — report generation, data entry, follow-up messages, status updates — consume hours that should go toward growth, guest experience, and strategic decisions.

### 6. AI is difficult to integrate

Enterprise AI capabilities exist, but they require technical teams, custom integrations, and fragmented point solutions. SMB operators cannot access intelligent automation without disproportionate cost and complexity.

## Key Features

### Mission Control Dashboard

The central command center for the entire business. Real-time KPIs, alerts, and status indicators pulled from every connected system — occupancy, revenue, orders, ad performance, tasks, and more — in one configurable view. Designed to answer *"How is the business doing right now?"* in seconds.

### AI Assistant

A conversational intelligence layer grounded in live business data. Operators ask questions in natural language — *"What was our RevPAR last week compared to the same period last year?"* or *"Which products had the highest return rate this month?"* — and receive accurate, contextual answers without building reports or switching tools.

### Daily Briefing

An automated morning summary delivered to each user role: key metrics, anomalies, pending tasks, and recommended actions for the day. Replaces the manual ritual of checking five dashboards before work begins.

### Knowledge Vault

A centralized repository for business documents, SOPs, contracts, templates, and institutional knowledge — searchable, AI-indexed, and accessible from anywhere in the workspace. Turns scattered files into an intelligent knowledge base the whole team can use.

### Task Manager

Cross-functional task tracking connected to business events. Tasks can be created manually, assigned to team members, or triggered automatically by integrations — a new booking generates a housekeeping task; a low-stock alert generates a reorder task.

### Hotel Module

Purpose-built workflows for hospitality operators:

- Occupancy and revenue overview across properties
- Reservation sync from PMS and OTAs
- Guest communication hub
- Housekeeping and operations task automation
- Channel manager and rate parity visibility

### Commerce Module

Unified e-commerce operations:

- Order and inventory sync from Shopify, WooCommerce, and marketplaces
- Product performance analytics
- Fulfillment status tracking
- Returns and refund monitoring

### Marketing Module

Connected marketing intelligence:

- Campaign performance across Meta, Google, and email
- Spend vs. revenue attribution
- Audience and creative insights
- Automated campaign alerts and recommendations

### CRM

A lightweight, integrated customer relationship layer:

- Unified customer profiles from every connected channel
- Conversation history across WhatsApp, email, and booking platforms
- Segmentation and tagging
- Follow-up automation and reminders

### Finance Dashboard

Financial visibility without replacing accounting software:

- Revenue, expenses, and cash flow overview
- Invoice and payment status from Stripe, Razorpay, and accounting integrations
- Margin analysis by product, property, or channel
- Automated financial summaries and anomaly detection

### Analytics

Cross-system reporting and exploration:

- Pre-built reports for each vertical (occupancy, sales, marketing ROI, etc.)
- Custom report builder with drag-and-drop metrics
- Trend analysis, comparisons, and forecasting
- Exportable insights for stakeholders and investors

### Integrations Hub

A centralized management interface for all connected applications:

- One-click connector setup for supported platforms
- Connection health monitoring and sync status
- Data mapping and field configuration
- Webhook and API access for custom integrations

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target (Phase 1) |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | Unique users engaging with ORION workspace per day | 70%+ of subscribed seats active weekly |
| **Time Saved** | Self-reported and system-measured reduction in time spent on manual tasks and context-switching | 2+ hours saved per user per day |
| **Tasks Automated** | Number of workflows executed automatically via ORION per month | 500+ automated actions per account per month |
| **Revenue Growth** | Measurable revenue improvement attributed to ORION insights or automation | 10%+ revenue uplift within 90 days of onboarding |
| **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)** | In-product satisfaction score collected quarterly | 4.5+ out of 5.0 |
| **Subscription Renewals** | Percentage of accounts renewing at end of billing cycle | 90%+ annual renewal rate |

**North Star Metric:** Time saved per operator per day — the single measure that captures ORION's core value proposition of reducing complexity and amplifying human capability.

Industry by industry, market by market, ORION will connect the world's business software into one coherent, intelligent system — accessible to every organization ready to operate at a higher level.
